Welcome to Our Guesthouse in Brittany

Enjoy a peaceful stay in Domagné, perfectly located to explore Rennes, Vitré, and the beautiful Emerald Coast. But not only that not only that !

🏡 The Accommodation

Our fully furnished rental is designed to provide a warm and comfortable home away from home. Ideal for holidaymakers exploring Ille-et-Vilaine or professionals on the move, it features all the necessary amenities for a relaxing and independent stay.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 About Your Hosts

We are a French-Russian couple, both working for the french national postal service, and proud parents of three children. Hospitality and public service are part of our daily life. We look forward to welcoming you and can assist you in English, Ukrainian, Spanish, Russian and of course French if you want to practice.

🌿 Eco-Responsible Stay

We are committed to reducing our environmental footprint. Our house is equipped with photovoltaic solar panels for electricity, solar water heating, and a rainwater harvesting system for outdoor maintenance.
Our blog will show you how comited we are with sustainable developpment (#developpement durable in French)

📍 Perfect Location

Located in the quiet countryside of Domagné, you can easily radiate across the region: Vitré (20 min), Châteaugiron (10 min), Rennes (25 min), and the iconic Mont-Saint-Michel or Saint-Malo for a perfect day trip.
Check also our page Tourism (in french) to discover the hidden gems in the neighbouhood
